Rules & Regulations

General Rules

The CONSTRUMATOR application must be used responsibly and only for its intended purpose of assisting individuals in estimating construction materials. Users are required to provide accurate and truthful information when inputting data, as false or misleading entries may affect the reliability of the results.

Any misuse of the application, including unauthorized access, sharing of accounts, or attempts to manipulate its features, is strictly prohibited. All personal information and project data are kept confidential in accordance with the app's privacy measures, and users are not allowed to disclose or misuse the data of others.

Terms of Service

The developers reserve the right to suspend or terminate access for users who violate these rules. CONSTRUMATOR is provided as an aid for estimation and should not be relied upon as a substitute for professional judgment.

Continued use of CONSTRUMATOR signifies agreement with these terms. If you do not agree with any of these rules and regulations, please discontinue use of the application immediately.

These terms may be updated from time to time. Users will be notified of significant changes, and continued use after such notifications constitutes acceptance of the updated terms.
